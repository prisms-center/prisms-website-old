Overview

Here at PRISMS we are dedicated to developing an integrated suite of multi-scale computational tools. These tools will eventually be open sourced and available to the wider community. Below is a high level overview of our proposed architecture.

KS-DFT

Equation: \[ H\psi = E\psi \]

Output: \[ E^\mathrm{form},\;E^\mathrm{mig}\;\mathbb{C}_{ijkl} \]

Monte Carlo and Kinetic Monte Carlo

Input: \[ E^\mathrm{config}\\ Ppt growth \]

Equation (Monte Carlo): \[ Z = \sum \limits_\omega e^{-\beta E_\omega} \]

Equation (Kinetic Monte Carlo): \[ \frac{\partial P_j(t)}{\partial t} = \sum \limits_i \left(R_{i\rightarrow j}P_i(t) - R_{j\rightarrow i}P_j(t)\right) \]

Output: \[ E(x,F,S),\;\Gamma \\ Ordering, correlation functions\] \[ M,R \]

Phase field with FE, FD and Fourier series

Input: \[ E^\mathrm{gb} \] \[ E(x,F,S),\;\Gamma \]

Equation: \[ \frac{\partial c(x)}{\partial t} = \nabla\cdot\left(\Gamma\left(c(x)\right)\nabla\mu\right) \]

Output: \[ Nano-/micro-structure, \\Mean \space field \space dynamics \]

Continuum elastoplasticity with FEM

Input: \[ W(F^\mathrm{e}),\;\mathbb{C}_{ijkl} \] \[ \phi,\;\tau_\mathrm{Y},\;K(\bar{\varepsilon}^\mathrm{p}) \]

Equation: \[ \rho\frac{\partial^2u}{\partial t^2} = \nabla\cdot\tau + f,\quad \tau = \frac{\partial W}{\partial F^\mathrm{e}}F^{\mathrm{e}^\mathrm{T}},\quad F = F^\mathrm{e}F^\mathrm{p}\\ \phi(\tau^\mathrm{dev}) - \tau_\mathrm{Y} - K(\bar{\varepsilon}^\mathrm{p}) \le 0 \]

Phenomenological mass transport with FEM

Input: \[ Mean \space field \space dynamics \\ M,R\]

Equation: \[ \frac{\partial \bar{c}}{\partial t} = \nabla\cdot\left(M\nabla\bar{\mu}\right) + R \]

Atomistic constitutive laws

Input: \[ E^\mathrm{form},\;E^\mathrm{mig}\;\mathbb{C}_{ijkl} \]

Equation: \[ x,\; y \]

Output: \[ Twinning \\ m^\mathrm{twn}_\beta,\;n^\mathrm{twn}_\beta,\;\tau_{\beta_0}^\mathrm{twn} \] \[ \tau_{\alpha_0} \]

Discrete dislocation dynamics

Input: \[ Dislocation \space mobilities \]

Equation: \[ f^\mathrm{PK}_i = \epsilon_{ijk}\tau_{jm}b_m\xi_k \]

Output: \[ H^{\alpha\beta} \]

Crystal plasticity with FEM

Input: \[ Twinning\\ m^\mathrm{twn}_\beta,\;n^\mathrm{twn}_\beta,\;\tau_{\beta_0}^\mathrm{twn} \] \[ \tau_{\alpha_0} \] \[ H^{\alpha\beta} \] \[ H^\mathrm{twm}_{\beta\delta} \] \[ Nano-/micro-structure\]

Equation: \[ \tau_\alpha = m_\alpha\cdot\tau n_\alpha = \tau_{\alpha_0} + \sum_{\beta}H_{\alpha\beta}\gamma^\beta\\ \tau^\mathrm{twn}_\beta = m^\mathrm{twn}_\beta\cdot\tau n^\mathrm{twn}_\beta = \tau^\mathrm{twn}_{\beta_0} + \sum_ {\delta}H^\mathrm{twn}_{\beta\delta}\gamma^\delta \]

Output: \[ \phi,\;\tau_\mathrm{Y},\;K(\bar{\varepsilon}^\mathrm{p}) \]

Effective Hamiltonians

Input: \[ E^\mathrm{form},\;E^\mathrm{mig}\;\mathbb{C}_{ijkl} \]

Equation: \[ E(\sigma) = V_0 + \sum_{\alpha} V_\alpha\,\prod_{i\in\alpha}\sigma_i \] \[ \sigma_i = -1,\;\sigma_j = 1 \]

Output: \[ E^\mathrm{config} \] \[ W(F^\mathrm{e}),\;\mathbb{C}_{ijkl} \]
