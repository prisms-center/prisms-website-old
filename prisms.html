PRISMS Center

Welcome

The Predictive Integrated Structural Materials Science (PRISMS) Center is dedicated to the development of integrated multi-scale computations tools. These tools enable the prediction and analysis of microstructural evolution and mechanical behavior of structural materials.

View Our Multiscale Architecture

Materials Commons

We are developing a knowledge repository and virtual collaboration space for curating, archiving and disseminating information from experiments and computations as well as providing a collaborative platform for development of opensource code with active involvement of a broader PRISMS open source community. This repository is called Materials Commons and will be available at http://materialscommons.org.

Check back here and at the Materials Commons site for updates. We are working hard to build up our repository of computational and experimental data and results.
